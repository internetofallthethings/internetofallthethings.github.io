About Internet Of All The Things

Here at Internet of All The Things.com, I wanted to blog about things related to the internet-of-things. For me, this means every fucking thing on the internet. Software is on the internet. SCADA systems are on the internet. Embeded devices, exploits, people, data -- it's all on the internet of everything.

Right now, the best way to get in touch is via my twitter account @InternetOfAll. Otherwise email me at "thoughtleader at internetofallthethings.com." Thanks for stopping by.
